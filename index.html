Hello! My name is Emile Fleming. I grew up in a small college town in southern Ohio. I went to school for Industrial Design in Cincinnati at DAAP and moved to Seattle in 2013.

In the past I worked in the furniture industry and my hobby was (sort of) writing code. Now that I'll be a web developer I'll need a new hobby. Maybe furniture? I used to have a big loft in Pioneer Square filled with crap furniture from Goodwill, now I have a tiny one bedroom filled with crap furniture from Ikea. One of these days I'll get some nicer stuff but my girlfriend designs interiors so we can kind of work it.

I spent way, way too much time on this site with the idea that it might become my personal/portfolio page. I'm sure I'll learn that I did everythign wrong, throw it out, and start fresh though. Still, it was a lot of fun!
